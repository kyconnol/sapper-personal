An ever-evolving one-pager on ideas from music

My condensation of observations, and ideas from music

 

Why does music impact us humans so deeply? I think it's because the parts of music resemble the human experience:

  • The unique combination of otherwise generic notes, forming a melody, give a song its individuality
  • The relationship of other notes to the melody, forming harmony, give a song its beauty (or ugliness, or happiness, etc.)
  • The time overwhich it's all shared, forming a rythm / tempo, give a song its irrevocable preciousness
